WhatsApp Channels: The evolution & devolution of communication

WhatsApp rolled out "channels" worldwide on September 13, 2023, following the popularity of a similar feature in Telegram introduced 7 years prior. Through channels, users can subscribe to one-sided chat feeds and receive updates from individuals or organizations directly to their devices.
